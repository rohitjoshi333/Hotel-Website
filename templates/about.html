Our Story

Hotel New Chetan was established in 2019 with a vision to provide exceptional hospitality and create memorable experiences for our guests. Located in the heart of the city, we have quickly become a preferred destination for travelers seeking comfort, luxury, and authentic service.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our service - from our elegantly designed rooms and world-class dining to our professional event spaces and personalized guest care. We believe that hospitality is not just about providing accommodation; it's about creating a home away from home.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Hotel New Chetan, we believe in responsible hospitality that protects our environment and supports our local community. Our sustainability initiatives include:

  • Energy-efficient lighting and appliances throughout the property
  • Water conservation programs and eco-friendly amenities
  • Waste reduction and recycling initiatives

  • Supporting local artisans and community projects
  • Digital-first operations to reduce paper consumption
